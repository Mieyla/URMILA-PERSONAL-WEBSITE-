MY EDUCATION

Education Journey.~`

I began my educational journey by attending SK Sungai Burung, where I studied for 4 years.
Followed by LKTP Bukit Sagu 2 for another three years. After completing primary school, I continued my secondary education at SMK Sungai Isap Murni at the age of 13.
There, I dedicated myself to my studies and successfully completed my SPM with commendable results of 4A, 2B, and 1C.
Currently, I am pursuing my studies at Universiti Teknologi MARA (UiTM) Kelantan, Machang branch, taking a Diploma in Information Management under the Faculty of Computer Science. I continue my journey with confidence and a strong will to succeed.
